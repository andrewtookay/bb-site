About Us

At Bubble Breaker, we believe social media should do more than just entertain—it should challenge, inspire, and connect us in meaningful ways. Too often, we get stuck in echo chambers, surrounded by ideas that reinforce our beliefs without encouraging growth.

We’re here to change that.

We are building on the foundation of blockchain technology, with decentralization standing at our core. Our unique incentive system rewards well-reasoned arguments, diverse opinions, and fresh ideas. Whether you’re discovering new interests or breaking out of old habits, we provide a space where meaningful conversations thrive.
